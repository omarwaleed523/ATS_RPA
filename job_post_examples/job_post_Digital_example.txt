Job Title: Digital Media Specialist
Job Description: We are seeking a talented Digital Media Specialist to manage and grow our digital presence across various platforms. The successful candidate will create and implement digital marketing strategies, oversee content creation, and optimize campaigns to increase brand awareness and engagement.

Responsibilities:

Develop and execute digital media strategies across platforms such as social media, websites, and email campaigns.
Create and curate engaging content, including graphics, videos, and written posts, that align with the company’s brand and objectives.
Manage and monitor social media channels, responding to customer inquiries and interactions.
Analyze digital media performance using analytics tools and adjust strategies to improve engagement, reach, and ROI.
Collaborate with marketing, design, and content teams to ensure cohesive digital campaigns.
Stay updated on digital media trends and emerging platforms to keep the brand competitive.
Required Skills:

Strong understanding of digital marketing strategies and social media platforms (e.g., Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn).
Experience with content creation tools such as Adobe Creative Suite or Canva.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
Proficiency in using analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, social media insights) to measure campaign success.
Ability to manage multiple projects and work under tight deadlines.
Experience:

2+ years of experience in digital marketing or media management.
Education:

Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Digital Media, or a related field.
